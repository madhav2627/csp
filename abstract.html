ABSTRACT

The main objective of this project is to conduct a survey on the number of people availing government schemes in an area. For this project, we selected AC Nagar, SPSR Nellore District for conducting the survey. We have surveyed fifty houses in the specified locality and enquired about the number of people who are availing government schemes. These government schemes are very helpful to many people in surviving their lives. This project is useful to identify the people who are eligible and not availing government schemes.
